Response Time

We respond to all enquiries within 24-48 hours. If you haven't heard from us, please check your spam folder or reach out via WhatsApp.

Availability

We book only a limited number of weddings each season to ensure every couple gets our full attention. Peak season (October-February) dates fill up 6-12 months in advance.

Investment

Our packages start at ₹1,50,000 for intimate celebrations and go up based on coverage, events, and deliverables. Custom proposals are sent after our initial conversation.

Destination Weddings

We love traveling for weddings. For destinations outside Mumbai, travel and accommodation are either included or quoted separately based on location and logistics.
